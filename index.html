For Teachers — Internal Reference

One world. Eleven guardians. A journey that grows with our children.

Each realm carries its own little world: a theme, a guardian to protect, characters to become, and ideas to bring it to life on stage. The youngest guardians bring pure magic. From Year 4, each realm also carries a real United Nations Global Goal — a cause worth protecting — with concrete ways to show it in the performance.
